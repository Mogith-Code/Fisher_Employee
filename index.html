Features

Built to Handle Every Detail

Experience a comprehensive suite of workforce operations built to eliminate admin bottlenecks and foster transparent team dynamics.

Attendance Tracker

Monitor employee status, remote sign-ins, and leave allocations automatically in real-time, syncing directly to global calendar views.

Smart Payroll Automations

Compute complex hourly tracking rates, tax deductions, bonuses, and reimbursements directly with one-click direct deposit actions.

Shift & Scheduling

Build optimized, drag-and-drop calendars in seconds. Let team members swap shifts easily, minimizing communication gaps and scheduling disputes.

Talent Analytics

Gain insights into workforce attrition rates, productivity peaks, shift utilization factors, and overtime payout allocations instantly.

Self-Service Portal

Allow employees to manage profiles, apply for leave, retrieve check stubs, and register shifts without direct admin interventions.

Enterprise Integration

Sync effortlessly with corporate tools like Slack, MS Teams, Google Workspace, and major payroll databases via secure API endpoints.
